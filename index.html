Harvey is a very skilled senior mental health clinician with over thirty-five years of dynamic and varied international experience working with individuals, couples, families and children.

With post graduate training in both clinical psychology and child and family development, Harvey is equipped to help families constructively address and resolve challenging situations with integrity, and in a manner which is ultimately in the best interests of all of the members of the family.

A strong advocate for children with a deep understanding and appreciation of how children both perceive and respond to crisis, Harvey is committed to assisting families and children address and resolve challenging developments in a manner that has positive and long-term resolution.
